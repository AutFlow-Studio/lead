// Four conversion levers most pages leave completely untouched

What a Page Built for Paid Traffic Actually Requires

Message-to-market match from line one — the page must continue the exact conversation your ad started. Any gap between ad promise and page headline causes immediate bounce and wasted spend.

Friction-free conversion path — every element either moves the visitor toward the conversion action or it doesn't belong on the page. No distractions, no navigation, no competing CTAs diluting intent.

Trust architecture calibrated to cold traffic — objection-handling copy, social proof, and risk-reversal elements placed where cold visitors need reassurance before they'll commit.

Load speed as a conversion variable — a one-second delay measurably reduces conversion rate. Every page is built for sub-second load on mobile, where Meta and TikTok traffic actually arrives.
