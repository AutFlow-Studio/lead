About
// The designer behind the work

I'm Anes Hass — a web designer who works exclusively with skilled creative independents who've outgrown the platforms they started on. Not beginners. Video editors with client rosters, motion designers with real showreels, UX professionals with strong case studies, and brand designers who charge proper rates — people whose work is genuinely excellent, but whose online presence still signals "available gig worker" instead of "trusted creative authority."

The pattern repeats itself: a freelancer with years of strong work, legitimate results, and clear expertise — but their digital identity is split across Upwork, Behance, a Notion page, and maybe a link-in-bio. High-budget clients and creative directors don't read that as "versatile." They read it as unconsolidated. And they move on to the person who looks like they've built something real.

That's exactly the gap I close. I build personal brand websites that position creative freelancers as the premium professionals they already are — so the right clients find them, trust them immediately, and reach out with real budgets and the right context.

The Real Problem
// Why your best work isn't getting you your best clients

Platforms are designed to keep you competing on price. Always.

Upwork. Fiverr. Behance. LinkedIn. Every one of these puts you in a list next to thirty other creatives, filters you by rate, and strips your work of all the context that makes it powerful. Your portfolio doesn't speak for itself in an algorithm. It needs a stage. It needs framing. It needs to be the only thing a client sees when they're deciding whether to trust you with a $10,000 project.

// The four things your current online presence is missing

What separates a "nice portfolio" from a client magnet

A positioning statement that earns premium rates — the moment someone lands on your site, they should immediately understand what you do, who you do it for, and why you're worth it.

Work presented with context, not just aesthetics — results, process, and decision-making. High-value clients need to understand your thinking, not just see your output.

Social proof that answers real objections — testimonials and case studies structured to close the deal before you ever have a call.

A direct pipeline you own completely — your domain, your inbox, your rules. No algorithm updates, no competitor listings, no commission skimmed off your revenue.
