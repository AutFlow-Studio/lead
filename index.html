About

// The designer behind the work

I'm Anes Hass — a web designer who works exclusively with skilled freelancers and independent professionals who've outgrown what Upwork can offer. Not beginners. Consultants, designers, copywriters, and agencies with strong track records, verified results, and a quiet ceiling — because the platform they built on was never designed to let them charge what they're actually worth.

The pattern is consistent: a developer, strategist, or creative with dozens of five-star contracts and a highly rated profile — but their entire professional identity lives inside a marketplace that signals "vetted contractor" to every client who lands there. Enterprise clients and high-budget decision makers don't trust that context when they're allocating serious budget.

That's the gap I close. I build personal brand websites that position you as a high-caliber independent professional — someone worth hiring at rates that reflect your actual ability, not Upwork's competitive price dynamics.

The Real Problem

// Why your skills aren't translating into higher rates

Upwork Was Never Built to Make You Look Premium.

The platform commoditizes talent by design. Clients arrive with budgets pre-set and comparison tools at hand. You compete next to equally rated freelancers at a fraction of your rate. No matter how strong your work is, the context signals "hire from a marketplace" — not "retain a senior expert." A personal brand website removes you from that context entirely. It's the difference between being one of many search results and being the undeniable authority in your field.

// What a high-caliber freelancer's website actually needs

Four Things an Upwork Profile Will Never Give You

A positioning statement that commands premium rates — the first line a client reads should immediately establish your expertise and who you serve.

A portfolio that tells the story behind the work — results, context, and process. Clients allocating $5,000+ decide based on understanding, not bullet points alone.

Social proof on your terms — your best testimonials and case studies, structured to answer the questions serious buyers ask before they reach out.

A direct path to contact — outside any platform's control — your domain, your pipeline. No algorithm changes, no account restrictions, no 20% service fee.
