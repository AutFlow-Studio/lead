// 01 — Aesthetic Clinic

Luxury Aesthetic Clinic Website

High-end medical aesthetic studio · Facial treatments, body contouring, anti-aging injectables

// Overview

This clinic had strong word-of-mouth and a loyal patient base — but their website was failing new visitors entirely. First impressions were generic, the brand didn't communicate luxury, and the path to booking was buried across a poorly structured site. The brief: design and build a complete premium website that communicates high-end positioning within three seconds and guides every visitor toward one outcome — booking a consultation.

The Problem

A generic, template-based website was eroding trust before patients even read the copy. No credibility anchors, no clear value proposition, no single conversion path. Visitors were leaving without contacting the clinic.

The Solution

A luxury-positioned website with a clear booking path on every page, trust-engineered copy that speaks to a high-value patient, and a design language borrowed from the premium beauty and skincare industry — not a generic medical template.

// Conversion Strategy

The website was built around the ATICA framework — Attention, Trust, Identification, Consequence, Action. The homepage captures intent immediately with a bold headline addressing the patient's core desire. Social proof and credential markers appear before the first scroll. The booking CTA reappears across every key page, each in a different context to catch visitors at different stages of intent.

// Website Features
►Luxury homepage design with a headline engineered to speak directly to high-value patients seeking discretion and results.
►Before/after results grid with treatment-specific social proof to reduce risk perception before booking.
►Doctor credentialing block — qualifications, certifications, and authority markers formatted to project medical trust.
►Objection-handling FAQ — answers the five questions hesitant patients ask themselves before they'd ever call a clinic.
►WhatsApp-first booking — reduces friction by letting patients initiate contact in a channel they already use daily.
►Mobile-first responsive build — optimized for mobile-dominant clinic searches with sub-2-second load time across every page.
// Results
+73%Consultation requests
3.2×Conversion rate lift
-58%Bounce rate drop
// 02 — Laser Clinic

Laser Hair Removal Clinic Website

Modern laser clinic · Full-body laser hair removal, skin rejuvenation, IPL treatments

// Overview

The clinic was generating traffic through Instagram ads targeting women aged 22–38, but visitors were landing on a generic clinic homepage with no connection to the ad creative. The conversion gap was massive. The brief: build a complete website with a dedicated laser hair removal experience that speaks to exactly the patient the ads are targeting — addressing pain fears, explaining the process clearly, and getting them to book in under 60 seconds.

The Problem

High ad spend was sending warm, ready-to-book traffic to a generic website that created confusion, triggered pain anxiety, and gave visitors no clear next step. Cost-per-booking was unsustainably high.

The Solution

A laser-specific website with copy that directly neutralizes the two biggest objections — pain and permanency — while building urgency through social proof and a friction-reducing instant-booking flow.

// Conversion Strategy

The entire website was structured around objection removal at every scroll depth. Pain anxiety is addressed on the homepage. Permanency is validated with a numbered treatment timeline in section two. Price hesitation is reframed with a cost-per-session comparison. Every piece of copy was written to lower perceived risk and raise perceived reward — moving the patient from "I'm interested" to "I'm booking now."

// Website Features
►"Zero pain. Permanent results." homepage statement with a sub-headline that immediately answers the patient's first question before they scroll.
►5-step treatment timeline — visual walkthrough of what actually happens session by session, eliminating the unknown and building confidence.
►Pain-level comparison chart — positions laser against waxing and threading using sensation language that resonates with the target patient.
►Verified review block with authentic patient testimonials from Google and Instagram, displayed in a trust-maximizing format.
►Sticky booking bar — always visible on mobile, allowing the patient to book the moment they're ready without hunting for a CTA.
►Ad message match — homepage copy and imagery designed to feel like a seamless continuation of the clinic's Instagram creative.
// Results
+91%Bookings from ads

// 03 — Dermatology Clinic

Dermatology Clinic Website

Medical dermatology center · Acne, eczema, skin analysis, prescription skincare, chemical peels

// Overview

Unlike cosmetic clinics, dermatology patients are making a medical decision — and they require a fundamentally different kind of trust before they'll book. This clinic was losing patients to competitors who appeared more established online, despite having a more qualified doctor. The brief: build a complete website that communicates medical authority so clearly that choosing this clinic over a competitor feels like the obvious, responsible decision.

The Problem

The clinic's online presence projected neither authority nor credibility. Patients searching for an actual dermatologist — not a beauty clinic — couldn't tell from the website that this was a qualified medical practice, and were choosing competitors.

The Solution

A clinically-styled website where every visual and copywriting decision was made to signal medical professionalism — from the typography system to the doctor biography to how patient conditions are described in precise, reassuring clinical language.

// Conversion Strategy

The website was structured to build authority first, then ask for the booking. The first two pages establish medical credibility through credentials, certifications, and clinic affiliation. Condition-specific pages then address the patient's exact problem — acne, eczema, or pigmentation — with language that sounds like a doctor, not a marketer. By the time a booking CTA appears, the patient has already decided this is the right clinic. The experience closes with urgency and a low-friction WhatsApp consultation option.

// Website Features
►Medical authority header — doctor name, specialization, years of practice, and certification logos positioned above the fold, before anything else.
►Condition-targeted pages — dedicated content for acne, skin aging, eczema, and pigmentation, each speaking directly to what brings the patient to the site.
►Clinical process breakdown — a step-by-step explanation of the consultation, diagnosis, and treatment plan process that reduces uncertainty and builds confidence.
►Patient outcome stories — written testimonials with skin condition context, formatted to feel genuine and medically grounded rather than promotional.
►Reassurance close — a final section that directly addresses the fear of being judged, sold to, or misdiagnosed, lowering the emotional barrier to booking.
►Clean clinical design language — white space, precise typography, and a restrained color palette that signal precision and professionalism without feeling cold.
